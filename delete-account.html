Delete Your AnklePath Account & Data

AnklePath (developer: Timothy Wasswa) • Last updated: 29 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your AnklePath account and the personal data associated with it. AnklePath is an educational ankle-recovery app. You can request deletion at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Request deletion inside the app

  1. Open AnklePath and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll to the Legal section and tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm by tapping Email request. This opens a pre-filled message to our support address.
  4. Send the email. We will verify and process your request.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

  1. Email 24.stom.wasswatimothy@gmail.com from the address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my account".
  3. Include the name on your AnklePath profile so we can locate your data.

What data is deleted

When your request is verified, we delete the personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Your account record and sign-in identifier (email/name from Apple, Google, or email sign-in, or your anonymous guest ID).
  • Your recovery inputs: injury type and timing, symptoms, walking ability, and recovery goals.
  • Your pain and swelling check-ins and exercise-completion history.
  • Your profile photo, if you added one.

What data may be kept, and for how long

DataRetention after deletion
Account & recovery data (above)Deleted within 30 days of a verified request.
Aggregated / anonymized analytics that can no longer identify youMay be retained for product statistics.
Records we must keep for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasonsKept only as long as required by law, then deleted.
Tip: Signing out of AnklePath also clears your locally stored progress on that device. A deletion request additionally removes your data from our servers.
