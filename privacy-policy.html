Privacy Policy

AnklePath • Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the AnklePath mobile application ("AnklePath", "the app", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using AnklePath you agree to this policy.

Who we are

AnklePath is operated by the AnklePath developer (Timothy Wasswa). For any privacy question or request, contact us at 24.stom.wasswatimothy@gmail.com.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your free-text health notes for advertising, and we avoid sending detailed health notes to our diagnostics tools.

How your information is used

  • Provide core features: your personalized plan, exercise tracking, pain/swelling logs, and education.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the app's reliability and experience.
  • Respond to your support requests.

Service providers we use

We share limited information with trusted providers who process it on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) — account sign-in and storage of your plan, logs, and onboarding answers.
  • PostHog — product analytics.
  • Sentry — crash and error monitoring.
  • RevenueCat / app-store billing — used only if and when paid subscriptions are activated in a future version (currently inactive; AnklePath is free).

These providers may process data outside your country. We rely on their standard contractual and security protections.

Data retention

We keep your account and recovery data while your account is active. Signing out removes your locally stored progress on the device. You can request deletion of your account and associated server-stored data at any time (see below).

Your choices and rights

  • Access / correction: You can edit your name and recovery inputs in the app.
  • Account & data deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing 24.stom.wasswatimothy@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account". We will process verified requests within 30 days.
  • Photo library: Choosing a profile photo is optional and only happens when you tap to add one.
  • Depending on your region (e.g. EEA/UK GDPR, California CCPA), you may have additional rights to access, port, or erase your data.

Children

AnklePath is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Security

We use industry-standard measures from our infrastructure providers to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

Medical disclaimer

AnklePath provides educational guidance and general recovery information. It is not medical advice and does not replace assessment, diagnosis, or treatment by a qualified clinician.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
